About

I am a passionate and driven software developer eager to embark on a career in the tech industry. With a solid foundation in Java, Web Technologies, Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and more, I am keen to join an organization that values innovation, integrity, and a forward-thinking approach to technology.

I hold a Master's degree in Information Science with a concentration in Information Systems from the University of North Texas, preceded by a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from DRK College of Engineering and Technology, affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad. My academic journey has provided me with a deep understanding of both the theoretical and practical aspects of technology and information systems.

As I continue to grow in my career, I aspire to take on leadership roles, contributing to impactful projects and driving innovation within the industry.

What i do

Front-end

As a javascript developer, I have worked extensively on both the frontend and backend of web applications, developing a strong skill set in creating dynamic and user-friendly interfaces as well as robust server-side logic. On the frontend, I am proficient in web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and have used Angular to build responsive single-page applications that provide a seamless user experience. My focus on UI/UX design ensures that the applications I develop are not only functional but also intuitive and accessible across different devices and browsers.

Back-end

On the backend, I have utilized Java and frameworks like Spring Boot to develop scalable and maintainable applications. My experience includes designing and implementing RESTful APIs to facilitate smooth communication between the frontend and backend systems. I have also worked extensively with databases, particularly MySQL and Hibernate, to manage data efficiently and ensure optimal performance. Additionally, I have implemented authentication and authorization protocols using Spring Security to safeguard application access.

Cloud Services

In the realm of cloud services, I have leveraged platforms like Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Microsoft Azure to deploy and manage applications in the cloud. My experience includes configuring and managing services such as EC2, S3, and RDS, as well as utilizing Docker and Kubernetes for deploying microservices-based applications. This comprehensive experience has equipped me with the ability to develop, deploy, and maintain full-stack applications effectively.
